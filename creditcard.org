<h2 id="orgcdaedca"><span class="section-number-2">1.</span> Goals and Priorities</h2>
<div class="outline-text-2" id="text-1">
<p>
Do I want to earn cashback, travel rewards, or points?
</p>
<ul class="org-ul">
<li>I don't care as much about cashback. I want to accumulate a lot of points, and travel rewards would
be nice as well.</li>
<li>As I spend the most on food, I would like a strong bang-for-buck on food expenditures.</li>
<li>However, since I also spend a fair amount on travel, I would like to reap some travel rewards as well.</li>
</ul>


<p>
I am not considering the credit card's APR, since I will pay the balance in full every month.
</p>

<p>
The annual fee does not matter much to me, as long as I can make up the fee I pay with whatever
rewards and credits I'm offered. I want to be able to make up for the annual fee with my natural
spending habits.
</p>
</div>
